Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AiHome.

General

What is AiHome?

AiHome is a creative interior visualization app. It helps you restyle rooms, replace or rearrange furniture, recolor walls and floors, and export design previews.

AiHome provides visualization only and is not a professional architectural or engineering tool. Consult qualified professionals for construction, safety, or compliance decisions.
How does AiHome work?

You choose or capture room photos and set style preferences. AiHome processes images to generate design previews and suggestions that you can export or share.

Privacy & Safety

Is my data safe with AiHome?

We prioritize your privacy. No account is required. We do not use in‑app advertising or third‑party analytics by default. Images and inputs you choose to process may be sent to backend services to generate results.

Do you show ads or use third‑party analytics?

No. AiHome does not use in‑app advertising or third‑party analytics by default.

What personal information do you collect?

We do not require an account. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you directly contact us. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Features

What can I do with AiHome?
  • Apply styles and restyle interiors
  • Replace or rearrange furniture virtually
  • Recolor walls, floors, and materials
  • Export previews and share design results
Can I export my results?

Yes. You can export preview images or design results to share. After export, handling of the exported files is your responsibility.

Permissions

What permissions does the app need?
  • Photos: Needed to select images for design processing.
  • Camera (optional): If you capture new photos within the app.
  • Notifications (optional): If you enable tips or guidance.

Troubleshooting

Design results look distorted
  • Use well‑lit photos with clear room boundaries.
  • Avoid extreme angles; capture straight‑on shots when possible.
  • Ensure the subject area is not heavily occluded.
Processing is slow or fails
  • Check your network connection.
  • Try smaller image sizes or fewer concurrent tasks.
  • Update to the latest app version.
How do I export my results?

Open the export feature and choose your preferred format or destination. After exporting, keep your files secure and share only with trusted parties.
