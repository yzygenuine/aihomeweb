Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how AiHome (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use the AiHome mobile application (the “App” or the “Service”).

AiHome is designed to help you redesign and visualize interiors, apply styles, replace furniture, recolor walls and floors, and export design results. We prioritize your privacy and handle your images and design inputs responsibly.

Key Points

  • No account is required to use AiHome.
  • We do not use in-app advertising or third-party analytics by default.
  • Images and design inputs you choose to process may be transmitted to our backend services to generate results. Exports are initiated solely by you.
  • Notifications, if any, are used solely for optional guidance or tips you enable.

Information We Handle

Photos and Design Inputs You Provide

The App allows you to upload or select photos of interiors and provide design preferences. These are used to generate visual results, style suggestions, and previews.

Permissions

  • Photos: Access to your photo library is required to select images for design processing.
  • Camera (optional): If you capture new photos from within the App.
  • Notifications (optional): If you enable guidance or tips.

Export and Sharing

You may choose to export your design results (e.g., files or share options) to send them to collaborators or professionals. Exports are initiated solely by you. After export, the handling of that copy is your responsibility.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email, address) unless you directly contact us.
  • We do not use third‑party advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs by default.

Use of Information

Images and inputs are used to provide core features such as visual redesign results, previews, suggestions, and exports. If you contact us for support and share information with us, we will use it only to respond to your inquiry.

Data Storage and Security

Your images may be transmitted to backend services to generate results and may be cached on your device for performance. Please protect your device with a passcode and keep your operating system updated. If you delete the App or your device data, locally cached results may be lost unless you export or back them up.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

International Use

Your use of the App may be subject to privacy and health data regulations in your region. We do not transfer your health data to our servers. If you choose to export data, that exported copy may be processed by services of your choice according to their terms and privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above and publishing the updated policy in the App resources or website.
